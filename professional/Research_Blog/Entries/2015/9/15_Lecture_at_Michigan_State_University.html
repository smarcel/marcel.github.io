Lecture at MSU

 
 

I was invited to give a lecture on Sep 14 2015 at the Lecture Series of the Computer Science and Engineering department of the Michigan State University.

He presented our work on spoofing/anti-spoofing and visited the Biometrics Research Group of Prof. Anil K. Jain (h-index 149) — University Distinguished Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Michigan State University.


More info about the talk, can be found here: Spoofing, Anti-Spoofing and Reproducible Research in Biometrics
