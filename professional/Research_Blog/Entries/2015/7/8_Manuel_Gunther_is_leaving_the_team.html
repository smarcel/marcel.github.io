Good bye Manuel

 
 

After nearly 4 years in the biometrics group at Idiap working on face recognition, Manuel ( https://www.idiap.ch/~mguenther/ ) is leaving the team. Manuel is one of the great contributor to BOB ( http://idiap.github.io/bob/ ) and developed the BOB face recognition library ( https://pypi.python.org/pypi/facereclib ).


Manuel goes to the University of Colorado at Colorado Spring to work with Terry Boult ( http://www.vast.uccs.edu/~tboult/index.html ) on a project funded by the IARPA JANUS program ( http://www.iarpa.gov/index.php/research-programs/janus ).


No doubt that the expertise Manuel gained over those years in the Biometrics group at Idiap will be useful for this project.
