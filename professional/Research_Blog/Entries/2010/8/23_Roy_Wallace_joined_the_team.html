Roy Wallace joined the team

 
 

Roy Wallace joined the biometrics group at Idiap on August 16 to work in the Marie Curie BBfor2 project on “Bayesian Networks for Face Recognition”.


Welcome Roy !


Roy completed his PhD at the Speech, Audio, Image & Video Technology lab at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT), Australia. Through his doctoral work in the field of spoken term detection, he has been involved in patent applications, and has performed commercial evaluations of his research. He has also established research collaborations with the Norwegian University of Science and Technology, and Fraunhofer in Germany. In 2008 he enjoyed an internship at Microsoft Research Asia in Beijing, publishing his work there on unsupervised adaptation for speech transcription.
