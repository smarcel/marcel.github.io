Two MOBIO papers accepted at ICPR

 
 

Two papers from the EU FP7 MOBIO project have been recently accepted to the upcoming International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR):

  1. A. Roy and S. Marcel. “Crossmodal Matching of Speakers using Lip and Voice Features in Temporally Non-overlapping Audio and Video Streams”.

  2. In IEEE International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR), 2010 (to appear).

  3. N. Poh, J. Kittler, S. Marcel, D. Matrouf and J.-F. Bonastre. “Model and Score Adaptation for Biometric Systems: Coping With Device Interoperability and Changing Acquisition Conditions”. In IEEE International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR), 2010 (to appear).
