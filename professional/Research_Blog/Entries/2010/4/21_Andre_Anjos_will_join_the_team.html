André Anjos will join the team

 
 

In June 2010, André Anjos will join the biometrics group at Idiap to work on the “Anti-Spoofing in Face Recognition” project.


Welcome André !


André Anjos started studying electronics and computers in 1994, when he joined the Electronics Engineering School (http://www.del.ufrj.br) at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (http://www.ufrj.br). He obtained a Ph.D. in Signal Processing in 2006 with a work on High-Energy Physics Particle Detection using Neural Networks. In 2004, he joins CERN (http://www.cern.ch), Switzerland to work on the development and construction of the Trigger and Data Acquisition systems of the ATLAS Experiment. His domains of interest are pattern recognition and applied computing.


You can follow this link to André's homepage (http://andreanjos.org) for more information, his CV and a complete list of publications.
