A center for biometric excellence

 
 

The Swiss Center for Biometrics Research and Testing is a center for biometric excellence established to advance biometric security technologies, mainly by facilitating collaboration between industry and academia. Its core missions are:

  1. •to carry out high quality research

  2. •to train talented engineers and researchers

  3. •to foster technology transfer from academia to industry

  4. •to propose evaluation and testing services in the domain of biometric security.
